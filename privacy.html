Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. It is Notify - Meeting Reminders's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our application.

1. Information We Collect

Notify - Meeting Reminders is designed to be privacy-first. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to our servers.

Calendar Data

Notify - Meeting Reminders requests access to your calendar solely to display your upcoming events and provide reminders within the app. This data is:

  • Read directly from your device's local calendar store (EventKit) or fetched directly via official Google and Microsoft APIs.
  • Processed entirely locally on your Mac.
  • Never uploaded to any external server or cloud service by us.

2. Data Storage

All app preferences (like theme, refresh interval, and enabled calendars) are stored locally on your device using standard macOS user defaults.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking services, or advertising SDKs. The app functions entirely offline (except for syncing your calendars with iCloud/Google/etc., which is handled by macOS system services, not by Notify - Meeting Reminders).

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub Issues page.
