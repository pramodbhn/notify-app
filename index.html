Purpose of the App

Notify - Meeting Reminders is a macOS menu bar application designed to help users manage their daily schedules efficiently. It connects to your existing calendar accounts (such as Google Calendar) to fetch and display your upcoming events directly in the Mac menu bar. Its primary purpose is to ensure you never miss a meeting by providing timely, customizable reminders and quick access to meeting links without needing to open a full calendar application.
