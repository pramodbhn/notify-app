Notify - Meeting Reminders

Your Calendar, One Click Away

Notify - Meeting Reminders ensures you never miss an important meeting again. Living quietly in your macOS menu bar, Notify keeps your schedule visible and your meetings just one click away.

Purpose & Connectivity

Notify - Meeting Reminders is a macOS menu bar application designed to help users manage their daily schedules efficiently. The app integrates seamlessly with your existing macOS Calendar accounts and now supports connecting your Google and Microsoft calendars directly via official APIs.

How we use your Data: Notify - Meeting Reminders uses Google and Microsoft OAuth to request read-only access to your calendar events. This data is processed entirely on your local machine to provide timely meeting reminders and quick access to meeting links. We do not store, collect, or share your calendar data or any personal information with our servers or any third parties.
